About David

As a single dad of a medically complex, nonverbal ASD child, I understand both the unique challenges and beautiful moments of special needs families.

Whether your child is neurodivergent, medically complex, or simply needs extra patience and understanding—I create a judgment-free, flexible photography experience that celebrates your child exactly who they are.

I know that feeding tubes, communication devices, sensory tools, and mobility aids are part of your child's story. I don't ask you to hide them—I honor them.

Sessions are flexible, follow your child's lead, and there's zero pressure for "perfect" poses. We work at your child's pace, take breaks as needed, and capture the authentic beauty of your family.
